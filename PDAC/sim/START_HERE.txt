THE CORE FINDING
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

GPU PDE Solver Has MIXED Results:

GOOD NEWS (30% of chemicals):
  ✓ O2, ArgI, NO are at correct magnitudes
  ✓ Diffusion/decay mechanisms working (TGF-β shows correct decay trend)
  ✓ Solver is numerically stable for most chemicals
  ✓ Per-voxel uptake code looks correct

BAD NEWS (70% of chemicals):
  ✗ IL-2 is 100,000x too high (34.4 µM vs 0.01 pM expected)
  ✗ IL-10 and VEGF-A are 10,000x too high
  ✗ IFN-γ and IL-12 are 50-100x too high
  ✗ CCL2 diverges to NaN at step 206 (total solver failure for that chemical)
  ✗ Initial conditions seem wrong (chemicals pre-loaded at t=0)

VERDICT: Per-voxel uptake fix was incomplete or has other issues preventing
correct concentrations. Likely root causes: uptake collection not working,
secretion rates wrong, or initial placement issues.

THE THREE CRITICAL ISSUES
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. CCL2 SOLVER DIVERGENCE (BLOCKER)
   ├─ What: Entire grid outputs NaN starting at step 206
   ├─ Why: Preconditioner/operator mismatch or CG convergence failure
   ├─ Impact: Cannot use CCL2 results after step 205
   ├─ Files: PDAC/pde/pde_solver.cu lines ~305 (preconditioner), ~487 (operator)
   └─ Fix: Check diagonal computation, operator sign, CG tolerance

2. IL-2 MAGNITUDE (CRITICAL)
   ├─ What: 34.4 µM instead of <0.01 pM (100,000x too high!)
   ├─ Why: Uptake collection not working OR secretion rates 1000x too large
   ├─ Impact: T cell IL-2 signaling biochemistry broken
   ├─ Files: PDAC/pde/pde_solver.cu line 268, PDAC/pde/pde_integration.cu
   └─ Fix: Debug uptake arrays, verify secretion rates from XML

3. INITIAL CONDITIONS (CRITICAL)
   ├─ What: IL-10 and VEGF-A start at 32-37 µM (not from zero)
   ├─ Why: Agent initialization pre-loads PDE with chemicals
   ├─ Impact: Immunosuppression/angiogenesis signals wrong from start
   ├─ Files: PDAC/sim/initialization.cu
   └─ Fix: Verify PDE starts empty, chemicals only from agent secretion
